## ⚠️ Critical finding — NAP duplicate

A stale Yellow Pages listing **"Ongles Lien Pham Nails — 3035 boul Le Carrefour, Laval"** shares Sans Souci's exact address. A duplicate/old business listing at the same address **splits local ranking signals and confuses Google's entity resolution**. Action: claim, merge, or request removal in Phase 1. This is likely the single highest-impact local fix.
